Reporting succulents is a relatively simple process. Here are three easy steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Time for Repotting
The best time to repot your succulents is during their active growing season, which is typically in spring or early summer.

Step 2: Prepare the New Pot and Soil
Select a new pot that is slightly larger than the current one. Ensure it has drainage holes to prevent water-logging.

Step 3: Repotting Process Here's how to repot your succulents:
a. Water the succulent a day or two before repotting.
b. Gently tap the old pot or squeeze it to loosen the soil.
c. Inspect the roots and prune any damaged, rotten, or excessively long roots.
